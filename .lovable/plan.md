## Page Flow

### 1. Hero Section
- Beautiful romantic header with animated hearts or subtle sparkles
- A warm welcome message like "Our Love Story" or her name
- Elegant typography with soft, romantic colors (pinks, reds, rose gold)

### 2. Timeline / Love Story Section
- Vertical timeline showing your journey together
- Each milestone has:
  - Date/time marker
  - Placeholder image spot (you'll add your photos later)
  - Caption area for your memories

- Smooth scroll animations as you move down the page

### 3. Photo Gallery
- A beautiful grid gallery section
- Placeholder images with "Add your photo" indicators
- Clean, elegant layout that showcases your memories together

### 4. The Big Question Section
